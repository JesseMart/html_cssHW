Brazilian Jiu Jitsu (BJJ for short) has been part of my life
for about 3 years now. Its been a ride to train and learn
from people from many backgrounds of fighting and cultures.
A family is formed once you start at a gym, it takes time
but its guaranteed. This part of my life has brought pain from torn muslces to broken ribs and dislocated fingers, however its still worth it being able to get stronger as time progresses.
